History of Beef Wellington

It's assumed the name of Beef Wellington comes from the Irish Arthur Wellesly, the first Duke of Wellington. Wellesly, also known as the Iron Duke, was a military leader from the time of Napoleon. He was also the man who finally defeated Napoleon at the Battle of Waterloo.
