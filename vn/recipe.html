Bánh mì

Recipe

Checklist

For Marinade:

  • Fish sauce
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Shallot
  • Brown sugar
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Pork tenderloin
  • Dark soy sauce

For Pickled Vegetables:

  • Water
  • Sugar
  • Coarse salt
  • Carrots
  • Daikon
  • White vinegar

For Sandwich:

  • French baguette
  • Greek Yogurt
  • Sriracha
  • Paté
  • Cucumber
  • Cilantro springs
  • Jalapeño
  • Fried egg

Preparation

  1. Using a large bowl, combine all marinade ingredients. Add the pork tenderloin, stir to coat the meat evenly and let the marinate sit for about 30 minutes.
  2. Bring the vinegar, water, sugar and the salt to a boil in a small sauce pan to make the pickled vegetables. Stir to dissolve the sugar and add the carrots and daikon. Remove the pan from the heat and let the mixture cool down to room temperature.
  3. Preheat a grill or grillpan over a high-heat. Grill the marinaded pork and turn this once until the meat reaches 140F, which takes about 4 minutes. When done, remove the pork from the grill and let it rest for at least 5 minutes. When rested, slice the meat thinly.
  4. Preheat the broiler with the rack in a upper third position. Cut the banquette in half, lengthwise and place the cut side up on the oven rack. Broil the bread until toasted, it should be browned around the edges. This takes about 2 minutes, you can remove the bread from the oven.
  5. Combine yogurt or mayo and sriracha in a smaller bowl. Spread this mixture evenly on the bottom half of the banquette. Spread the pate on top and place cucumber spears, pork, pickled carrots & daikon and cilantro & jalapeños. You can add a fried egg on the dish if you wish to, top sandwich of with the top of the bread and cut into portions to serve.
