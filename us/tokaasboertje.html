History of Hamburgers

In the 19th century Hamburg became known for its beef. The beef was often ground and formed into burgers and seasoned. Because there wasn't cooling available at the time the beef had to be cooked as soon as possible. Because if this it was very expensive to get Hamburg beef steak outside of the region. If people tried to replicate the recipe they often used meat of a lesser quality.
