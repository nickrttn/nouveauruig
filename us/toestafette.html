History of Hamburgers

The family of Oscar Weber Bilby claims the first ever hamburgers on buns were served on granddad Oscar's farm in 1891. They say Oscar is the first to come up with serving burgers on buns, but admit burgers on a slice of normal bread were probably introduced before.
