Mozzarella Hamburgers

Mozzarella Hamburgers

Recipe

Recipe


Checklist

  • 500g Ground beef
  • 70g Tomatoes
  • 1,5 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 100g Mozzarella
  • 4x Burger buns
  • 250g Mushrooms
  • 1 clove Garlic
  • 1 teaspoon Salt
  • 2 teaspoons Pepper
  • 1 tablespoon Olive oil

Preperation

  1. Mix the beef, italian seasoning, salt and pepper in a large bowl. Shape the mixture into 8 patties. Make out of 60g mozzarella a ball. Press it flat into 3 patties of 1/2 inch-diameter. Repeat this with the remaining cheese. Place the cheese patties on top of 4 beef patties. Top them off with the remaining beef patties and pinch the edges together to seal.
  2. In a 10-inch skillet, heat the oil over a medium-high heat. Add your mushrooms and let it cook for 4 minutes. Steer often until they are slightly browned. Then add garlic and cook the mixture 1 minute longer. Remove from heat.
  3. Heat gas or charcoal grill. Place the patties on a medium-heated grill and cover them. Cook for 11 to 13 minutes, turning them once. Let the patties cook until the meat thermometer reads 160F when inserted in the center of the patties. Slice the buns during the last 2 minutes of cooking, place them with the cut side down on the grill until toasted.
  4. Place burgers on the bottom halves of the buns, add the mushroom mixture on top of them and cover the burger with the top half of the bun. The burgers are ready to be served.
